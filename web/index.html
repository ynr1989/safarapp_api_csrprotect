In Safe hands with Safar

Your passport to private healthcare

Relax in the comfort of knowing that should illness occur, you will be in safe hands with Safar.
Our service makes it easy for you to access online medical advice and speak to a UK-based GP, all from the comfort of your home or any European country.

No Hidden Fees

A one-off, annual payment gives you complete access to all features and includes your first consultation at no additional charge. Any further consultations are then charged on a pay-as-you-need basis.

Why not have the Safar features at your fingertips? Add medical records to your account, refer to location specific travel advice and book your medical consultations on the go by downloading the Safar app onto your mobile phone.
